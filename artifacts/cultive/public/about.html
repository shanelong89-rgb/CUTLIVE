What This Is

Not an app. Not a newsletter.

A person inside WhatsApp who looks things up, goes to the thing, and tells you what's worth your time — before it happens.

You text us what you're in the mood for. We send you three picks. You go out. That's the whole thing.

"We track them down, verify the details, and put them all in one place — so you never find out about the perfect event the morning after."

The Origin

Why we built this

Hong Kong has more happening on a Tuesday night than most cities have on a Saturday. But finding it means following fifty accounts, checking six group chats, and still hearing about the good stuff on Monday morning.

We got tired of that. So we started going ourselves — and sharing what we found with friends.

FoundedHong Kong, 2024
FormatWhatsApp-first, human-curated
ModelNo paid placements, no scraping
How It Works

Every event reviewed by a real person.

We don't scrape listings. We don't take payment to recommend something. If it's here, someone went, or someone we trust went, and it was worth it.

We tell you when something's actually free — not "free with purchase." We remember what you like.
