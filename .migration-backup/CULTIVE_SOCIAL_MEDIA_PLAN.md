# CULTIVE Social Media Launch Plan
**Goal:** Build hype, find curators, grow email list
**Platforms:** Instagram, Reddit, WhatsApp, Email

---

## CONTENT PILLARS

### 1. Event Previews (60% of content)
**What:** Highlight specific events with insider angle
**Format:** 
- Event flyer/image
- Date/venue
- "Why this is worth your time" (1 sentence)
- Link to site

**Example:**
```
📍 Mihn Club, Wan Chai
🗓️ This Friday, 11PM

The techno collective is back with their first summer session. 
Expect: heavy bass, no phone policy, actual dancing.

🔗 Full details + more events: cutlive20.vercel.app

#hongkongnightlife #techno #wanchai
```

### 2. Curator Spotlights (20%)
**What:** Feature the people submitting events
**Format:**
- "Meet [Name], our [music/arts/nightlife] curator"
- What they do/why they know the scene
- "They find the events so you don't have to"

**Goal:** Humanize the platform, attract more curators

### 3. Behind the Scenes (15%)
**What:** Building CULTIVE, editorial decisions, rejected events
**Format:**
- Screenshots of submissions queue
- "This week we rejected 8 generic club nights"
- "Our criteria: exclusivity, curation, local"

**Goal:** Build trust, show quality standards

### 4. Community/UGC (5%)
**What:** Repost user content, event photos
**Format:**
- Reshare attendee photos (with credit)
- "Who's going to [event] tonight?"
