# CULTIVE — Product & Platform Overview
### For Press, Media & Internal Marketing Use

---

## What Is CULTIVE?

CULTIVE is Hong Kong's curated cultural events platform — a web app and native mobile app built to surface the best of what's happening in the city, from underground music nights and gallery openings to members-only experiences most people never hear about.

We exist because Hong Kong's cultural scene is vibrant, scattered, and chronically under-documented. Great events get buried in Instagram stories, WhatsApp forwards, and Eventbrite pages no one finds. CULTIVE brings them together in one place, editorially curated and community-powered.

**Website:** cultive.city
**Mobile App:** iOS & Android (via Expo)

---

## The Core Idea

Hong Kong has incredible culture. The problem isn't that events don't exist — it's that finding them requires knowing the right people. CULTIVE fixes that.

We combine two things that rarely go together: editorial taste and community intelligence. Every event on the platform is reviewed before it goes live. But the tips come from the community — anyone can submit an event, and if it makes it through review, it goes live with their name on it.

The result is a feed that feels curated, not algorithmic. No noise. No sponsored filler. Just what's actually worth your time.

## What the Platform Does

### For Users

**Discover**
A clean, editorial-style events feed updated continuously. Filter by category (Music, Arts, Nightlife, Food & Drink, Wellness, and more), by district (Central, Wan Chai, Tai Hang, etc.), or by timeframe (Today, This Weekend, This Month). Events are sorted intelligently — what's coming up appears first, ongoing multi-day events stay visible, and recently ended events linger for a grace period so you don't miss context.

**Event Pages**
Every event has a full detail page: title, category, date and time, venue with a direct Google Maps link, pricing, and a rich description. From there you can save it, share it (natively on iOS, or via X, LinkedIn, or a copy link), RSVP directly, or buy tickets.

**Save & Come Back**
Bookmark anything. Your saved events sync across web and mobile. Get a reminder when something you saved is coming up soon. On mobile, you can add saved events directly to your device calendar.

**Submit an Event**
Two ways to contribute:
1. **Instagram Quick-Submit** — paste an Instagram post or Reel URL. Our system extracts the event details automatically and queues it for review.
2. **Manual Form** — fill in title, date, venue, category, price, description, image, and ticket link yourself.

Approved submissions are credited to their profile — recognition for the people who know what's worth sharing.

**Inbox**
A personal notification centre. You're notified when your submission is approved or rejected, and reminded when a saved event is coming up. Clean, no spam.

**Account & Profile**
Sign up with email or Google. Your profile shows your membership date, submission count, saved event count, and submission history with live status tracking (Pending Review / Approved / Rejected).

**Exclusive & Members-Only Events**
Some events on CULTIVE are gated. These are drops, private previews, and invite-level experiences surfaced only to CULTIVE members. This is the layer the platform is building toward — a trusted inner circle for Hong Kong's cultural scene.

### For the Community

CULTIVE is designed to surface the people who actually know what's happening. The people who know about the hidden jazz bar pop-up in Sham Shui Po or the private gallery opening in Wong Chuk Hang are the editorial backbone of the platform.

Every submission goes through human review before it goes live. That's intentional. It keeps quality high, and it means the feed stays trustworthy.

---

### For Event Organisers

Getting listed on CULTIVE means reaching an audience that is actively looking for exactly what you're doing. Unlike social media, where posts decay in 24 hours, a CULTIVE listing stays discoverable for the full run of your event.

Submission is free. The review process is fast. And because our users are self-selected culture seekers — not passive scrollers — the audience quality is high.

## The Technology (Plain English)

CULTIVE is a full-stack platform built to modern standards:

- **Web app** at cultive.city — works on any browser, fully responsive and optimised for mobile browsers
- **Native mobile app** for iOS and Android — built with React Native / Expo, feels native on both platforms
- **Real-time database** — events and submissions are live, not cached static pages
- **Smart event sorting** — our algorithm accounts for multi-day events, late-night events (events starting after 9pm get an extended window), and grace periods for recently ended events so the feed always feels current
- **Instagram enrichment** — submitted IG links are automatically parsed and enriched with event details, reducing the friction of contribution to a single paste
- **Push notifications** — mobile users get real-time inbox alerts
- **Google Sign-In** — one-tap authentication on both web and mobile, no password required
- **Image uploads** — event flyers and photos upload directly to cloud storage

The platform is built to scale. The architecture separates the API, database, web frontend, and mobile app as independent services — meaning each can grow independently as the user base grows.

## The Vision

CULTIVE is building the cultural operating system for Hong Kong.

**Phase One — The Platform** *(live now)*
A curated, community-powered events discovery platform across web and mobile. Human editorial review, smart discovery, saved events, and community submissions.

**Phase Two — Ticketing & Credits**
In-app ticketing for paid events, with QR code check-in and seamless RSVP management for organisers. Alongside this, a contributor credit system: community members who submit approved events earn credits redeemable against tickets purchased through CULTIVE. The value stays in the ecosystem — rewarding the people who feed it.

**Phase Three — The Membership Layer**
Tiered access, exclusive drops, and a verified inner circle of people who shape Hong Kong's culture. The best events on CULTIVE will be CULTIVE-first — not because anyone is locked in, but because the audience here is worth more than anywhere else.

The long-term bet is simple: own the discovery layer, own the ticketing layer, and the rest follows.
